Oxford Brookes Masonic Society

Oxford Brookes Masonic Society is a society for students at Oxford Brookes University who are interested in learning more about freemasonry. We are run by volunteers from two university scheme lodges in Oxfordshire part of the Honourable Fraternity of Ancient Freemasons (HFAF) and the United Grand Lodge of England (UGLE) representing womens and mens freemasonry respectively.
